In the year 2154, the Resources Development Administration (RDA) established a massive mining operation on the distant moon of Pandora. Their primary objective is to extract a highly valuable room-temperature superconductor known as Unobtanium. The RDA coordinates all of its mining and military operations from a heavily fortified complex called Hell's Gate.

To protect their assets from the hostile wildlife and the indigenous Na'vi population, the RDA relies on a robust private military force. The chief commander of security forces at Hell's Gate is Colonel Miles Quaritch, a seasoned and ruthless military veteran.

Meanwhile, the Avatar Program takes a different approach. Scientists genetically engineer Na'vi-human hybrid bodies, called Avatars, which are operated via neural link by human drivers. Paraplegic former Marine Jake Sully is recruited to drive an Avatar. His mission is to infiltrate the local Na'vi tribe, known as the Omaticaya clan, and convince them to relocate. The Omaticaya live in a massive, ancient tree that sits directly on top of the richest deposit of Unobtanium on the moon. This sacred site is located only a few miles away from the tribe's most revered spiritual location, the Tree of Souls.
